Sign up now!

Pre-test 22-24 January

  • We are looking for students with excellent logical, problem-solving, and programming skills; no previous knowledge on cybersecurity topics is required.

    You can check your preparation with the online pre-test which will be open for three days. You can start the pre-test at any time from February 22 through February 24, 2021.

    Some examples are publicly available for your training.

  • See example pre-tests
Logic quiz 2 February Morning

  • The admission test will take place simultaneously at each site on February 2, 2021, and it is reserved to participants who did a good job at the pre-test. For information about hours/logistics, please refer to the the university of your choice at venues page.

    The admission test is split in two parts. In the morning, in phase 1, you will encounter an online quiz (60 mins), similar to the one of the pre-test.

    Some examples are publicly available for your training.

  • See example logic quizzes
Programming test 2 February Afternoon

  • Phase 2, in the afternoon, is the programming phase and it will last for 3 hours. It is reserved for the participants who did the best job in the morning. The short list of students admitted to phase 2 will be announced in the early afternoon.

    By combining the results of the two phases we will define the final ranking: the top-20 students for each site will be selected to form the team for the edition 2021 of CyberChallenge.IT.

    Texts of the programming exercises are publicly available, but you need to log-in to submit the solutions.

  • See example programming tests
